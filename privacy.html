隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 22 日

一函春尊重你的隐私。应用以本地处理为核心，不要求注册账号，不包含广告，也不使用第三方分析或行为追踪服务。

我们不收集的数据

开发者不会收集、上传或出售你的姓名、联系方式、设备标识、使用记录、图片内容、文件名称或创作内容。

图片与创作内容

你导入的图片、抠图结果、贴纸、标签和文件夹作品仅在你的 Mac 上处理和保存。应用不会把这些内容上传到开发者服务器。如果你主动启用了 macOS 或第三方提供的云盘同步，相关文件可能由该服务按照其隐私政策进行同步，这不属于一函春的数据收集。

文件访问权限

只有在你主动选择文件夹、App、磁盘或图片后，应用才会访问该目标，用于读取图片、保存作品、应用图标或恢复默认图标。应用不会主动扫描或读取其他未选择的个人文件。

购买信息

订阅和永久购买由 Apple 的 App Store 与 StoreKit 处理。开发者不会获得你的银行卡或付款账户信息。应用只接收 Apple 提供的购买状态，用于判断 Pro 权益是否有效。

第三方服务

应用仅在购买、恢复购买和管理订阅时使用 Apple 提供的系统服务。Apple 对相关数据的处理受其自身隐私政策约束。

儿童隐私

应用不会有意收集任何用户（包括儿童）的个人信息。

政策更新

如果应用的数据处理方式发生实质变化，我们会更新本页面及生效日期。

联系我们

如有隐私问题，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系开发者。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

YihanChun respects your privacy. The app is designed around local processing. It does not require an account, display ads, or use third-party analytics or behavioral tracking.

Data We Do Not Collect

The developer does not collect, upload, sell, or share your name, contact details, device identifiers, usage history, image contents, file names, or creations.

Images and Creations

Imported images, cutouts, stickers, tags, and folder designs are processed and stored locally on your Mac. They are not uploaded to developer-operated servers. If you independently enable macOS or third-party cloud storage, those files may be synchronized by that service under its own privacy policy.

File Access

The app accesses a folder, app, drive, or image only after you select it, and only to import an image, save a creation, apply an icon, or restore the default icon. It does not proactively scan unrelated personal files.

Purchases

Subscriptions and lifetime purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. The developer does not receive your card or payment account details. The app receives only the purchase status supplied by Apple to determine whether Pro access is active.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple system services for purchases, purchase restoration, and subscription management. Apple's handling of related data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If the app's data practices materially change, this page and its effective date will be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through GitHub Issues.
