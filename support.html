使用帮助

一函春用于在 Mac 上创作文件夹图标，并将完成的设计应用到文件夹、App 或磁盘。

如何开始创作？

选择一个文件夹底板，调整颜色和材质，再加入内置贴纸、文字或自己的图片。你也可以直接上传图片，框选主体并抠图后加入画布。

如何移动或缩放贴纸？

先在画布或右侧图层列表中选中贴纸。拖动贴纸主体可以移动；拖动四角手柄可以缩放或旋转。贴纸还可以放在文件夹后层、夹层或前层。

图标没有立即刷新怎么办？

应用成功后请稍等片刻。如果 Finder 仍显示旧图标，可以重新打开目标所在的 Finder 窗口。对于桌面上的目标，也可以切换一次 Finder 显示或重新启动 Finder。

如何恢复默认图标？

在应用目标菜单中选择恢复默认图标，再选择需要恢复的文件夹、App 或磁盘。

购买后没有解锁 Pro？

确认 Mac 使用的是购买时的 Apple 账户，然后在购买页面点击“恢复购买”。如果交易仍在处理中，请等待 App Store 完成确认。

Support

YihanChun helps you design custom Mac folder icons and apply finished designs to folders, apps, or drives.

How do I start a design?

Choose a folder base, adjust its colors and material, then add built-in stickers, text, or your own images. You can also import an image, select its subject, remove the background, and add the result directly to the canvas.

How do I move or resize a sticker?

Select the sticker on the canvas or in the layer list. Drag the sticker itself to move it. Drag a corner handle to resize or rotate it. Stickers can be placed behind, inside, or in front of the folder.

Why has the icon not refreshed yet?

After the app reports success, allow Finder a moment to update. If the old icon remains, reopen the Finder window containing the target. For desktop items, refresh the Finder view or relaunch Finder.

How do I restore the default icon?

Choose the restore-default option from the target menu, then select the folder, app, or drive you want to restore.

Pro is not unlocked after purchase

Make sure your Mac is signed in with the Apple Account used for the purchase, then choose Restore Purchases on the purchase screen. Pending transactions unlock automatically after the App Store confirms them.
